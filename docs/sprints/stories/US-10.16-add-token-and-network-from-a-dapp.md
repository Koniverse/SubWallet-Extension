## Goal

Let a dApp ask the wallet to **add something** — a token, a network, or updated chain metadata — and
have the request land on the right screen instead of failing.

## Status

> **✅ done — all 5 rows below are settled and delivered.** It carries **no FR**: the connectors are
> FR-94 … FR-101; this story is the `wallet_watchAsset` / `wallet_addEthereumChain` family of
> requests they carry.
>
> **`version_shipped: 1.3.24` is a representative anchor, not the whole set** — the most recent
> constituent with a provable release.

## Scope

Folded in from the former one-issue-per-story maintenance ledger on 2026-07-24. Its own story because
these requests are the inverse of every other dApp interaction: the dApp is not asking the wallet to
*do* something with existing state, it is asking to *change* the wallet's own chain and token
registry — which is why they route to the add-token and add-network screens
([EPIC-4](../epics/EPIC-4.md)) rather than to a signing flow.
