## Goal

Widen what the shipped providers can do — one more venue, one more pair, one more fee token, one
more control — without adding a new handler. Each row belongs to a provider story that is already
`done`.

## Status

> **🚧 in-progress — nothing here has shipped.** All 5 rows below are **open on the tracker**: one
> `Implementing`, four `In Backlog`/`In Plan`. No acceptance criterion is ticked; `commit`, `sprint`
> and `version_shipped` stay empty until work lands in a release.

## Scope

This is an **improvement story on shipped ones**. Each row's home story —
[US-11.1](US-11.1-in-wallet-swap-via-chainflip.md),
[US-11.2](US-11.2-in-wallet-swap-via-hydration-dex.md),
[US-11.9](US-11.9-in-wallet-swap-via-optimex.md) — is `done`, and a `done` story may not carry an
open row ([AGENTS.md](../../../AGENTS.md) rule 9). They moved here on 2026-07-23.

It materializes **no FR**: the four FRs involved (FR-103, FR-104, FR-111, and the un-filed Axelar
case) are owned by the provider stories.

**Its tracker parent for the Bitcoin rows is the open umbrella
[#4517](https://github.com/Koniverse/SubWallet-Extension/issues/4517)** — *"Add support for 1-click
swap between native BTC & tBTC on Hydration"* — owned by [EPIC-11](../epics/EPIC-11.md) rather than
repeated as a row ([AGENTS.md](../../../AGENTS.md) rule 10).

**Split from [US-11.16](US-11.16-swap-flow-improvements.md)**: that story fixes what every provider
shares; this one extends what individual providers reach.

> **#3774 completes a three-issue arc that has been open since 1.3.2.** Chainflip got a *default*
> slippage (#3634) and then slippage *protection* (#3898), both in
> [US-11.1](US-11.1-in-wallet-swap-via-chainflip.md); letting the user set their own is the piece
> that never shipped. On a cross-chain broker settlement the wallet cannot tighten that tolerance on
> the user's behalf — only the user knows what they will accept.
>
> **#4595 is the last hop of the Bitcoin programme.** Chainflip swaps Bitcoin
> ([#4573](https://github.com/Koniverse/SubWallet-Extension/issues/4573), 1.3.51), Snowbridge brings
> tBTC to Hydration ([#4593](https://github.com/Koniverse/SubWallet-Extension/issues/4593), 1.3.54)
> — and swapping *between* the two representations on Hydration, which is what makes the round trip
> one click, is open.
>
> **#4709 is the third round of Hydration's fee-token question**, after #3465 (closed without
> shipping) and #4494 (shipped as *don't offer the list*) in
> [US-11.2](US-11.2-in-wallet-swap-via-hydration-dex.md). The omnipool can accept many assets as
> fee payment; the wallet has spent three issues deciding how much of that to expose, and is now
> adding them back one token at a time.
>
> **#1521 is the only row here for a provider that does not exist yet.** Axelar has been open since
> 2023 and has never been ruled on — unlike the five in
> [US-11.15](US-11.15-providers-evaluated-not-integrated.md), which were.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] **AC-1** — A user can set a custom slippage tolerance for Chainflip swaps (#3774).
- [ ] **AC-2** — Native BTC and tBTC can be swapped on Hydration, completing the one-click BTC round trip with #4573 and #4593 (#4595).
- [ ] **AC-3** — Hollar is selectable as a fee-paying token on Hydration (#4709).
- [ ] **AC-4** — The Optimex provider improvements scoped in #4796 are delivered, or the issue is closed with the reason recorded.
- [ ] **AC-5** — Axelar is either integrated as a swap provider with its own story under this epic, or #1521 is closed with the reason recorded — as the five providers in [US-11.15](US-11.15-providers-evaluated-not-integrated.md) were.
