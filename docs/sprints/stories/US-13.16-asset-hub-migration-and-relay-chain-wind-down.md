## Goal

Survive the **Asset Hub Migration** — the ecosystem event that moved balances and functionality off
the Polkadot and Kusama relay chains. For cross-chain transfer that meant stopping the routes that
pointed at the relay chains, re-pointing their parameters, and turning them back on once the
migration settled.

## Status

> **✅ done — all 4 rows below are settled**: 3 delivered, 1 closed without shipping. It carries
> **no FR**: AHM materialized no requirement — it removed the ground a requirement was standing on.
>
> **`version_shipped: 1.3.66` is a representative anchor** — the release that re-enabled the routes.
> The table is the full record.

## Scope

Folded in from the former one-issue-per-story maintenance ledger on 2026-07-23. Its own story rather
than rows in [US-13.12](US-13.12-xcm-route-and-channel-coverage.md), because these four are not
per-route work that happens to be about DOT and KSM: they are one **externally-imposed migration**
with a beginning, a disable, a parameter change and a re-enable. Read as ordinary channel coverage
they would be four unrelated tickets; read together they are an event the wallet had no say in.

The swap side of the same event is
[#4733](https://github.com/Koniverse/SubWallet-Extension/issues/4733) — *"Remove support swap to
DOT/KSM on Polkadot/Kusama"* — in [US-11.1](US-11.1-in-wallet-swap-via-chainflip.md).

> **The order of these rows is the whole story.** #4632 opened first — *prevent* cross-chain
> transfers from breaking — and closed **without shipping**. What shipped instead was #4787
> (1.3.63), re-pointing the DOT/KSM parameters, and then #4822 (1.3.66), *re-enabling* the routes.
> The wallet did not prevent the breakage; it turned the routes off, waited for the migration, and
> turned them back on. For a migration the wallet does not control, disable-and-restore is the
> honest strategy and prevention is not available.
>
> **Three releases separate the disable from the restore**, 1.3.63 to 1.3.66 — roughly six weeks in
> which relay-chain XCM simply was not offered.
>
> **#4836 is the WebApp doing the same parameter change in its own version space**
> ([AGENTS.md](../../../AGENTS.md) rule 1b), which is why it is `done` with `Shipped: —`.
