## Goal

Make dApp connectivity work **on the WebApp** — connecting to the extension, connecting other
wallets, and the connection-status behaviour of a surface that is a page, not a background.

## Status

> **✅ done — all 12 rows below are settled**: 11 delivered, 1 closed without shipping. It carries
> **no FR** — the connectors are FR-94 … FR-101; this story is the second surface they run on.
>
> **`version_shipped: 1.1.36` is a representative anchor, not the whole set** — the most recent
> constituent with a provable release. The WebApp has its own version space
> ([AGENTS.md](../../../AGENTS.md) rule 1b), which is why several rows carry `Shipped: —`.

## Scope

Folded in from the former one-issue-per-story maintenance ledger on 2026-07-24. Its own story because
the WebApp connects to dApps as a *page talking to the extension*, not as a background injecting a
provider — a different relationship with different failure modes.

The injected-account caching that this surface still owes is
[US-10.12](US-10.12-webapp-injected-account-caching.md) (#2286), and the open WebApp connection
improvements are in [US-10.17](US-10.17-open-dapp-connection-and-signing-improvements.md)
([AGENTS.md](../../../AGENTS.md) rule 9).
