## Goal

The work *around* dApp connectivity rather than in it: the in-app dApp directory, the integration
documentation partners follow, and the cross-browser QA of the connect surface.

## Status

> **✅ done — all 6 rows below are settled**: 4 delivered, 2 closed without shipping. It carries
> **no FR** and asserts coverage, not behaviour — a dApp listing and an integration doc materialize
> no requirement.
>
> **`version_shipped: 1.1.27` is a representative anchor, not the whole set** — the most recent
> constituent with a provable release; two rows ship in a CMS/doc, not a release.

## Scope

Folded in from the former one-issue-per-story maintenance ledger on 2026-07-24. Its own story because
these are **catalogue and process work, not code paths**: adding a dApp to the in-app list, keeping
the integration document current, and QA'ing the connect flow are the maintenance a connectivity
epic accumulates that never touches the connectors themselves.
