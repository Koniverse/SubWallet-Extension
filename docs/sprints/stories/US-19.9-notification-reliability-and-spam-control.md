## Scope

This is an **improvement story on a shipped one**. US-19.8 shipped the in-app notification centre
in **1.3.4** and is `done`; these two issues ask for things it did not deliver. It materializes
**no FR** — FR-159 is owned by US-19.8 — and carries no `version_shipped` until it ships.

**Why it is its own story and not rows in US-19.8.** A `done` story may not carry unfinished work
([AGENTS.md](../../../AGENTS.md) rule 9): rule 4 forbids it an unticked AC, and an open row in its
incremental-work table is the same claim through a different field.

**`commit` is filled while `version_shipped` is empty, and that is the honest pair.** The four
commits below are real and merged; `git tag --contains` returns **nothing** for all four, so no
release carries them. A version here would claim a shipment that has not happened
([D106](../../CONTEXT.md#d106-commit-names-what-made-the-capability-true--a-release-bump-made-nothing-true)).
