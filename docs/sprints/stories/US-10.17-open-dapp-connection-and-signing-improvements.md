## Goal

The dApp work that is asked for and not delivered — spread across connection, signing, error
messages and per-chain fixes — that no shipped capability story can carry without becoming a mix of
done and open rows.

## Status

> **🚧 in-progress — nothing here has shipped.** All 23 rows below are **open on the tracker**. No
> acceptance criterion is ticked, and `commit`, `sprint` and `version_shipped` stay empty until work
> lands in a release.

## Scope

This is the **open catch-all** for EPIC-10. Every capability and maintenance story in this epic that
is `done` shed its open rows here, because a `done` story may not carry an open row
([AGENTS.md](../../../AGENTS.md) rule 9). The rows come from many themes — connection lifecycle,
signing, authorization, the WebApp, add-token — and are held together only by being unfinished.

It materializes **no FR**. Themed open work with a natural home went there instead: Bitcoin
([US-10.5](US-10.5-bitcoin-dapp-connection-psbt.md)), TON
([US-10.6](US-10.6-ton-dapp-connection.md)), coexistence
([US-10.10](US-10.10-multi-wallet-coexistence-hardening.md)), WalletConnect
([US-10.11](US-10.11-walletconnect-session-and-dashboard-hardening.md)), the WebApp cache
([US-10.12](US-10.12-webapp-injected-account-caching.md)), and createTransaction
([US-10.9](US-10.9-dapp-createtransaction-api-rfc-6213.md)). What remains here is the unthemed
remainder.

> misleading error when the chain RPC is unreachable"* was `In Review` on the board, and the
> 2026-07-15 sync used it to move [US-10.11](US-10.11-walletconnect-session-and-dashboard-hardening.md)
> to `review` — but it is an EVM error-handling issue, not WalletConnect work. It belongs here, and
> US-10.11 is `in-progress` again as a result.
>
> **The signing remainder is here.** #3659 (*"support showing decoded txs from dapp"*), #3092
> (*"validate when the user sends to an EVM QR-signer account"*), #4311 (*"improve signing a
> transaction with a dApp on Cardano"*) — open signing improvements whose settled siblings are in
> [US-10.8](US-10.8-arbitrary-message-signing.md).

> *"allow an EVM dApp to import a network and tokens with a logo"* — adding assets from a dApp
> shipped, adding them *with branding* did not.
