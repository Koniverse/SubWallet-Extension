## Goal

Replace **one Ledger app per chain** with **one Ledger app for all of them**, and then keep it
current: adopt Zondax's Generic Polkadot app, migrate the accounts attached under the old apps, and
ship the runtime metadata every chain upgrade invalidates.

## Status

> **✅ done — all 14 rows below are settled**, delivered across releases 1.2.11 → 1.3.56 and later
> closes with no release of their own. It carries **no FR** — FR-144 is owned by
> [US-16.1](US-16.1-ledger-hardware-wallet-signing.md), whose AC-3 already asserts metadata-hash
> signing; this story is the platform migration that made that assertion hold for every chain at
> once.
>
> **`version_shipped: 1.3.56` is a representative anchor, not the whole set** — the most recent
> constituent with a provable release. The table is the full record.
>
> **The migration is done; the metadata is not a finished thing.** Rounds 2–5 shipped and the
> tracker holds no open round, but every Substrate runtime upgrade re-opens the work. `done` here
> means *no open row*, not *no future round*.

## Scope

Folded in from the former one-issue-per-story maintenance ledger on 2026-07-23. Split from
[US-16.4](US-16.4-ledger-network-and-app-coverage.md) on the axis that divides the work: **US-16.4
adds a chain, this story changes how every chain is reached.**

> **Five rounds of the same issue is the shape of this work.** #4480 and rounds 2–5 (#4653, #4715,
> #4828, #4876) are one recurring chore: a chain upgrades its runtime, the Generic app's metadata
> goes stale, the device can no longer render the extrinsic it is asked to sign. **Not one of the
> five carries a CHANGELOG line** — the rounds ship inside releases without being named, which is
> exactly why the ledger showed them all as `—`.
>
> **#4531 is the fallback for when a round has not happened yet**: block the networks whose runtime
> the current metadata does not cover, rather than let the device sign something it cannot display.
> It is the same trade as [US-16.6](US-16.6-ledger-signing-across-wallet-features.md)'s staking
> block — refuse rather than mis-sign.
>
> **#2453 was named in prose for a year and owned by nobody.** It sits in
> [US-16.1](US-16.1-ledger-hardware-wallet-signing.md)'s implementation notes as the Zondax generic
> app, and it carries its own 1.2.11 CHANGELOG line — but no story's table held it. It was recovered
> from [#4408](https://github.com/Koniverse/SubWallet-Extension/issues/4408)'s child list during
> this fold; the same list also confirms #3402, which the ledger had merged into #3307's row.
