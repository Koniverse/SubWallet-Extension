## Goal

A user can lend assets on Interlay to earn lending yield — supply an asset to the
money market, accrue interest, and withdraw — so that holders can earn on assets
that do not have a staking path, distinct from liquid staking.

## Background

Lending is a money-market yield, not a staking yield: the user *supplies*
liquidity to Interlay's lending pool, accrues a variable interest rate, and can
withdraw the supplied principal plus accrued interest. It is distinct from liquid
staking (FR-117) — there is no validator and no derivative staking token, just a
supply position and an accruing rate. The engine models it through the lending
branch of `BaseSpecialStakingPoolHandler`
([AD-22](../../ARCHITECTURE.md#architecture-decisions),
[US-2.3](US-2.3-earningservice-pool-handler-engine.md)). This story is the lending
*screen*: supply / withdraw flows and the supply position (supplied amount,
current supply APY, accrued interest), reusing the earning-list surface from
[US-12.1](US-12.1-native-nomination-staking-relay-and-parachains.md).

Materializes [FR-120](../../PRD.md#functional-requirements). **Retroactive** — already shipped.

## Acceptance criteria

- [x] **AC-1** — **Given** an Interlay account with a supported asset, **When** the
  user supplies an amount to the lending market, **Then** the supply extrinsic
  submits through the lending handler and the supply position (amount, supply APY,
  accrued interest) appears in the earning list.
- [x] **AC-2** — **Given** an active supply position, **When** the user withdraws
  part or all of the supplied amount, **Then** the withdraw extrinsic submits and
  the position state updates from the subject.
- [x] **AC-3** — **Given** a withdraw amount greater than the withdrawable balance
  (e.g. market utilization limits availability), **When** the user submits,
  **Then** the flow blocks with a clear availability error and submits nothing.
