## Goal

Record the wallet's **crowdloan** feature — contributing DOT/KSM to parachain crowdloans and showing a
user's contributions and rewards — from its introduction through its eventual removal. It materializes
**no FR**: crowdloan was never part of the earning FR map, and the Crowdloans tab was removed in 1.3.x.

## Status

> **✅ done — all 24 rows below are settled**: 23 delivered, 1 closed without shipping. This is a
> **retired feature** — the last row, #4920 (*"Remove the Crowdloans tab"*), ends it. `version_shipped`
> is a representative anchor across a feature that spanned many releases.

## Scope

Folded in from the former one-issue-per-story maintenance ledger on 2026-07-24. Its own story because
crowdloan is a **distinct feature**, not a facet of staking: it locks tokens in a parachain auction
rather than nominating a validator or joining a pool. The generator filed all 21+ crowdloan issues in
the earning ledger, and they cohere as one feature's whole life.
