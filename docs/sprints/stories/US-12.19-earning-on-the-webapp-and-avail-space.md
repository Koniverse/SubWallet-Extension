## Goal

Make staking, earning and crowdloan work **on the WebApp & Avail Space** — the earning list, the
Options and Pools screens, the staking-value calculation and the Crowdloans/Staking/History tabs — the
same earning model run as a page, not a background.

## Status

> **✅ done — all 39 rows below are settled**: 38 delivered, 1 closed without shipping. It carries
> **no FR** — staking and earning are FR-114 … FR-125; this story is the second surface they run on.
>
> **`version_shipped: 1.2.26` is a representative anchor, not the whole set** — the WebApp has its own
> version space ([AGENTS.md](../../../AGENTS.md) rule 1b), which is why several rows carry `Shipped: —`.

## Scope

Folded in from the former one-issue-per-story maintenance ledger on 2026-07-24. Its own story because
the WebApp runs the earning model as a *page*, with its own tabbed layout (#1840 Crowdloans/Staking/
History) and its own value-calculation and price bugs the extension's popup never hit.

The open WebApp earning work is in [US-12.17](US-12.17-open-earning-improvements.md)
([AGENTS.md](../../../AGENTS.md) rule 9).
