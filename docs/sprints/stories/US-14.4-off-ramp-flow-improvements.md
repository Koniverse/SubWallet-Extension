## Scope

This is an **improvement story on a shipped one**.
[US-14.2](US-14.2-fiat-off-ramp-sell-crypto-for-fiat.md) shipped the off-ramp in **1.3.56** and is
`done`; these two ask for things it did not deliver. It materializes **no FR** — the off-ramp
requirement is US-14.2's.

**Why its own story.** A `done` story may not carry unfinished work
([AGENTS.md](../../../AGENTS.md) rule 9): rule 4 forbids it an unticked AC, and an open row in its
incremental-work table is the same claim through a different field.

**#4453 is the substantive one.** It reports that selecting *Sell Token* **redirects the user out
of the popup** — the flow works, but not in one place. #3839 is smaller: order-ID display and UI
polish on the WebApp surface, which ships on its own version line
([AGENTS.md](../../../AGENTS.md) rule 1b).
