## Goal

Fix the QR-signing path where it fails in the field. Today that is one defect: a Polkadot Vault
account that can be attached and displayed but **cannot send funds**.

## Status

> **🚧 in-progress — nothing here has shipped.** The single row below is **open on the tracker** and
> `Follow Up` on Projects board #2; its acceptance criterion is not ticked. `commit`, `sprint` and
> `version_shipped` stay empty until the fix reaches a release.

## Scope

This is an **improvement story on a shipped one**.
[US-16.2](US-16.2-qr-signing-keystone-and-polkadot-vault.md) shipped QR signing for Keystone and
Polkadot Vault in **0.5.4** and is `done`; a `done` story may not carry an open row
([AGENTS.md](../../../AGENTS.md) rule 9), so this defect needed a story of its own.

**Why it took two folds to get here.** #3857 is a child of the cold-wallet umbrella
[#4175](https://github.com/Koniverse/SubWallet-Extension/issues/4175) and belongs to this epic, but
it was filed in the **Transactions** maintenance ledger. When the hardware ledger folded on
2026-07-23 this epic recorded it as owed and deliberately did not reach into an unfolded ledger to take it
([note](../../notes/2026-07-23.md#a-epic-36-maintenance--hardware-wallet-merged-into-epic-16)). That
ledger folded on 2026-07-24, and this story is where the debt is paid.

It materializes **no FR** — FR-145 and FR-146 are owned by
[US-16.2](US-16.2-qr-signing-keystone-and-polkadot-vault.md).

> **A QR device that can receive but not send is the worst half to have working.** The account
> attaches, the address displays, the balance is visible — and the one operation the device exists
> for does not complete. It is the mirror of
> [#43](https://github.com/Koniverse/SubWallet-Extension/issues/43) in
> [US-16.1](US-16.1-ledger-hardware-wallet-signing.md), which shipped *attach, show balance, receive*
> for Ledger in 0.3.1 and left signing for two releases later — except this time the gap is a
> regression, not a staged delivery.
>
> **Polkadot Vault contributed no rows to the hardware ledger at all** — all three of that area's
> QR issues were Keystone
> ([US-16.2](US-16.2-qr-signing-keystone-and-polkadot-vault.md)). Its only tracker issue is this
> one, and it was filed somewhere else.
