## Goal

The corridors and bridge options that were asked for and never delivered — one parachain pair, a
standing "more channels" request, two bridge providers outside the Polkadot ecosystem, a max-send
option on the Polygon bridge, and the wait experience after an XCM is submitted.

## Status

> **🚧 in-progress — nothing here has shipped.** All 6 rows below are **open on the tracker**: one
> `Follow Up`, five `In Backlog`. No acceptance criterion is ticked, and `commit`, `sprint` and
> `version_shipped` stay empty until work lands in a release.

## Scope

This is an **improvement story on shipped ones**.
[US-13.12](US-13.12-xcm-route-and-channel-coverage.md),
[US-13.4](US-13.4-polygon-unified-bridge.md) and
[US-13.15](US-13.15-xcm-ui-history-and-token-selection.md) are all `done`; these six issues ask for
things none of them delivered. A `done` story may not carry an open row
([AGENTS.md](../../../AGENTS.md) rule 9), so they moved here on 2026-07-23.

It materializes **no FR**.

**Its tracker parents are the two open umbrellas
[#4157](https://github.com/Koniverse/SubWallet-Extension/issues/4157)** — *"Review and add more XCM
channels"* — and **[#4188](https://github.com/Koniverse/SubWallet-Extension/issues/4188)** —
*"Support bridge without XCM"* — both owned by [EPIC-13](../epics/EPIC-13.md) rather than repeated
as rows ([AGENTS.md](../../../AGENTS.md) rule 10).

**Not here:** the reliability and claim-step defects, which are
[US-13.11](US-13.11-xcm-runtime-upgrade-paraspell-version-hardening.md); this story is about
**reach**, that one is about **whether what already exists still works**.

> **#1352 has commits in `v1.0.8` and is still open**, and both facts are true. Five commits carry
> `[Issue-1352]` and the earliest is contained in that tag — but the issue's title was later widened
> from *"Add support for USDT on more chains and update param for XCM on Astar"* to *"Add support for
> more XCM channels"*, and under the new title it will never close. A merged commit on an open issue
> is not a release ([AGENTS.md](../../../AGENTS.md) rule 1b-bis), so the row stays `—`.
>
> **#1352 and #4157 are the same standing request, three years apart.** *"Add support for more XCM
> channels"* (open, `Follow Up`) and its umbrella *"Review and add more XCM channels"* (open,
> `Follow Up`) — the corridor backlog has never been empty and has never been closed. The 36 rows in
> [US-13.12](US-13.12-xcm-route-and-channel-coverage.md) are what it has produced so far.
>
> **#1367 and #1378 are bridges outside Polkadot that never happened.** Octopus Network → NEAR
> (#1367) and the generic *"integrate bridge asset feature"* (#1378, a child of #4188 — *"support
> bridge without XCM"*) both predate Snowbridge. What the wallet built instead was
> [US-13.2](US-13.2-snowbridge-eth-asset-hub.md) … [US-13.5](US-13.5-across-protocol.md): five
> Ethereum-facing bridges and none toward NEAR.
>
> **#3288 is the missing half of an asynchronous transfer.** *"Improve the experience when users
> wait to receive tokens after XCM"* — the wallet tells the user the transfer was submitted and then
> has nothing to say until it arrives. Its bridge-side equivalents are the claim defects in
> [US-13.11](US-13.11-xcm-runtime-upgrade-paraspell-version-hardening.md).
