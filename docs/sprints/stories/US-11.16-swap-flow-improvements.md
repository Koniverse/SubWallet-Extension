## Goal

Finish the provider-neutral swap work that the shipped UI
([US-11.12](US-11.12-swap-ui-content-and-confirmation.md)) and quote pipeline
([US-11.13](US-11.13-swap-quote-pipeline-and-service.md)) did not deliver — chiefly **the fee**,
which four of these ten rows are about.

## Status

> **🚧 in-progress — nothing here has shipped.** All 10 rows below are **open on the tracker**: two
> `Implementing`, two `Ready to Implement`, six `In Backlog`/`In Plan`. No acceptance criterion is
> ticked, and `commit`, `sprint` and `version_shipped` stay empty until work lands in a release.

## Scope

This is an **improvement story on shipped ones**. US-11.12 and US-11.13 are both `done`; these ten
issues ask for things neither delivered. A `done` story may not carry an open row
([AGENTS.md](../../../AGENTS.md) rule 9), so they moved here on 2026-07-23.

It materializes **no FR** — the FRs in this epic are per-provider.

**Its tracker parent is the open umbrella
[#4227](https://github.com/Koniverse/SubWallet-Extension/issues/4227)** — *"[Round 3] Improve UX for
swap cross-chain"* — which owns #4194, #4260, #4349 and #4376 among others, and which is itself a
child of [#4156](https://github.com/Koniverse/SubWallet-Extension/issues/4156). Both are owned by
[EPIC-11](../epics/EPIC-11.md) rather than repeated as rows ([AGENTS.md](../../../AGENTS.md)
rule 10).

**Split from [US-11.17](US-11.17-swap-coverage-expansion.md)** on the axis this epic already uses:
that story adds a provider or a pair, this one fixes what every provider shares.

> **Four of ten rows are the fee, and it is the oldest unfinished thing in this epic.** #3160 (wrong
> fee), #3630 (wrong fee after reopening the popup), #4376 (no total at all), and #1951 (pay the fee
> in any asset, open since 2023). A cross-chain swap charges on both sides of the hop plus the
> provider's cut; nothing in the wallet has ever added those up into one number the user can check
> against what leaves their account.
>
> **#4390 is the quote refusing to forget.** Change the pair and the previous quote stays on screen
> — the failure mode [US-11.13](US-11.13-swap-quote-pipeline-and-service.md)'s pair-selection work
> (#2902, 1.3.30) made more likely by *narrowing* which pairs are offerable: a pair that disappears
> leaves its quote behind.
>
> **#4209 is the other half of [US-11.14](US-11.14-swap-pair-catalogue-and-tooling.md).** Its
> sibling #4208 — the same tooling for XCM pairs — closed; the swap half is still `Follow Up`. The
> catalogue moved to metadata, but nobody can yet author it by hand when the metadata is wrong.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] **AC-1** — The swap confirmation screen shows a total fee that survives reopening the popup and matches what leaves the account (#3160, #3630, #4376).
- [ ] **AC-2** — Changing the pair or provider clears the previous quote rather than leaving it on screen (#4390).
- [ ] **AC-3** — Swap and XCM amounts can be computed against the current account balance (#4194), and the low-liquidity warning is accurate per provider (#4411).
- [ ] **AC-4** — Swap pair files can be exported and authored outside the metadata pipeline (#4209).
- [ ] **AC-5** — Round-2 in-app swap content ships (#4349) together with the UX items scoped in #4260, or each is closed with the reason recorded.
- [ ] **AC-6** — Paying the swap fee in a non-native asset via the DEX pallet is either delivered or closed with the reason recorded (#1951).
