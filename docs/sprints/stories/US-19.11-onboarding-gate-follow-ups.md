## Scope

This is an **improvement story on a shipped one**. US-19.1 shipped the create/restore onboarding
wrapper in **1.1.68** and is `done`; these two ask for things it did not deliver. It materializes
**no FR** — FR-152 is owned by US-19.1.

**Why its own story.** A `done` story may not carry unfinished work
([AGENTS.md](../../../AGENTS.md) rule 9). Both issues were pulled out of the UI/UX ledger on
2026-07-22 with the rest of the onboarding-gate set.

**#2423 straddles two capabilities and is filed here deliberately.** Multilingual Terms of use is
i18n work ([US-19.2](US-19.2-multi-language-i18n-round-1.md)) on the *terms-acceptance gate*
(US-19.1). It is filed against the gate because that is the surface a user is blocked by; the
i18n machinery it needs already exists.
