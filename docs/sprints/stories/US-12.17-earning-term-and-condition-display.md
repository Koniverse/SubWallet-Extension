## Goal

Display earning terms and conditions to users before they commit to a staking/earning position, so users are informed of lock-up periods, withdrawal conditions, and other earning-specific constraints before depositing.

## Background

Before this story, users could enter an earning position (stake/lend/liquid-stake) without seeing the associated terms and conditions — lock-up duration, minimum staking period, unbonding time, withdrawal restrictions. This story adds a T&C display mechanism that shows these constraints at the point of commitment, giving users informed consent before locking funds.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] **AC-1** — **Given** the earning entry flow, **When** a user selects an earning option, **Then** terms and conditions are displayed before the user commits.
- [ ] **AC-2** — **Given** the T&C display, **When** the user has reviewed, **Then** a confirmation action is required to proceed.
- [ ] **AC-3** — **Given** the earning pool configuration, **When** T&C data is available, **Then** it is fetched and rendered correctly.
