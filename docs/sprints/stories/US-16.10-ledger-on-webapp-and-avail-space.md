## Goal

Bring the Ledger surfaces **outside the extension** up to what the extension already does — the
signing validation the WebApp is missing, its `@polkadot/api` version, and the two Avail Space
screens that still describe a Ledger flow they do not support.

## Status

> **📋 backlog — nothing here has shipped.** All four rows below are **open on the tracker** and
> `In Backlog` on the board; no acceptance criterion is ticked. `assignee`, `commit`, `sprint` and
> `version_shipped` stay empty until the work starts.
>
> **It will not carry an extension release when it ships.** The WebApp and Avail Space have their
> own version spaces ([AGENTS.md](../../../AGENTS.md) rule 1b) — which is why the shipped WebApp
> rows in [US-16.5](US-16.5-ledger-connection-and-account-discovery.md) and
> [US-16.7](US-16.7-generic-ledger-app-migration-and-metadata.md) sit at `Shipped: —` despite being
> `done`.

## Scope

This is an **improvement story on shipped ones**. Its four issues are the non-extension remainder of
work whose extension halves are already `done` — a `done` story may not carry an open row
([AGENTS.md](../../../AGENTS.md) rule 9), so they moved here on 2026-07-23.

It materializes **no FR** — FR-144 is owned by
[US-16.1](US-16.1-ledger-hardware-wallet-signing.md).

**Split from [US-16.9](US-16.9-ledger-connection-and-derivation-improvements.md) by version space**,
not by feature: these ship where the extension's version number does not reach.

- [ ] **AC-1** — The WebApp validates the account before signing with a Ledger account, matching the extension behaviour delivered by #3263 (#3340).

- [ ] **AC-3** — Avail Space's "connect Ledger" screen describes the flow it actually supports (#3594), and supports the Avail Recovery Ledger app already shipped on the extension by #3915 (#3994).
