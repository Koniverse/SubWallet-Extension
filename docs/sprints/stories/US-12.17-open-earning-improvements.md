## Goal

The staking- and earning-management work that is asked for and not delivered — spread across the
staking flow, validator selection, the earning UI and the WebApp — that no shipped capability story can
carry without becoming a mix of done and open rows.

## Status

> **🚧 in-progress — nothing here has shipped.** All 32 rows below are **open on the tracker**. No
> acceptance criterion is ticked, and `commit`, `sprint` and `version_shipped` stay empty until work
> lands in a release.

## Scope

This is the **open catch-all** for EPIC-12. Every capability and maintenance story in this epic that is
`done` shed its open rows here, because a `done` story may not carry an open row
([AGENTS.md](../../../AGENTS.md) rule 9). The rows come from many themes — the staking flow, validator
selection, earning content, the WebApp — and are held together only by being unfinished.

It materializes **no FR**. Themed forward work with a dedicated home went there instead: reward/APY
accuracy is [US-12.13](US-12.13-earning-reward-and-apy-accuracy-hardening.md) and earning performance
is [US-12.14](US-12.14-earning-performance-and-cache-hardening.md). What remains here is the unthemed
remainder.
