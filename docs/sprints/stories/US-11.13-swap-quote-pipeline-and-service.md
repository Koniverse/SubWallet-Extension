## Goal

The machinery behind the number: choose which pairs are offerable, ask every eligible provider,
rank the answers, validate what comes back, and keep the service that does it from rotting as
providers are added.

## Status

> **✅ done — all 10 rows below are settled**: 7 delivered, 3 closed without shipping. It carries
> **no FR** — the FRs in this epic are per-provider; the pipeline that fans out to them belongs to
> none of them individually.
>
> **`version_shipped: 1.3.79` is a representative anchor, not the whole set** — the most recent
> constituent with a provable release. The table is the full record.
