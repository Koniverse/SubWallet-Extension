## Goal

Get from **a plugged-in device to a usable account** — enumerate the addresses the connected Ledger
app can derive, show the ones the chain accepts, and attach them without duplicates. Everything
before the first signature.

## Status

> **✅ done — all 13 rows below are settled**: 11 delivered, 2 closed without shipping. It carries
> **no FR** — FR-144 is owned by [US-16.1](US-16.1-ledger-hardware-wallet-signing.md); this story
> holds the connect-and-discover half of that capability.
>
> **`version_shipped: 1.3.56` is a representative anchor, not the whole set** — the most recent
> constituent with a provable release. The table is the full record.

## Scope

Folded in from the former one-issue-per-story maintenance ledger on 2026-07-23. Separated from the
signing path because the failure modes do not overlap: a signing bug produces a bad signature, a
connection bug produces **no account at all**, and the two are debugged in different places.

The open half of this work — custom derivation paths, JSON import, and dropping the legacy
connector — lives in [US-16.9](US-16.9-ledger-connection-and-derivation-improvements.md), because a
`done` story may not carry an unfinished row ([AGENTS.md](../../../AGENTS.md) rule 9).
