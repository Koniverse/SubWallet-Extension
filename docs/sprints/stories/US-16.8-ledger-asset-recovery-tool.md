## Goal

Give a user a way back to assets that are **stranded but not lost**. A Ledger derivation account
holds only the asset its app was opened for; tokens sent to the wrong derivative address are still
on-chain and still the user's, but unreachable through the device. This is the standalone offline
tool that reaches them.

## Scope

Folded in from the former one-issue-per-story maintenance ledger on 2026-07-23. Its own story rather
than rows under [US-16.5](US-16.5-ledger-connection-and-account-discovery.md), because it is not
part of the extension's connect-and-sign surface at all: it is a separate artifact a user runs when
the device can no longer help them.

**Out of scope:** the derivation behaviour that strands the assets in the first place. That belongs
to [US-16.5](US-16.5-ledger-connection-and-account-discovery.md), and the open request for
user-chosen derivation paths — the change that would prevent the problem rather than repair it —
is [#3866](https://github.com/Koniverse/SubWallet-Extension/issues/3866) in
[US-16.9](US-16.9-ledger-connection-and-derivation-improvements.md).
