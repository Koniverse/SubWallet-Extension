## Goal

Make **one more route work** — the recurring per-channel, per-chain, per-asset work that follows
every XCM capability. Each row is a corridor between two chains that either did not exist or stopped
working.

## Scope

Folded in from the former one-issue-per-story maintenance ledger on 2026-07-23. **This is the
largest single story in the epic — 36 of its 115 rows** — and that is the finding: XCM's cost is not
the protocol, it is the corridor count.

Split from [US-13.13](US-13.13-xcm-channel-operations-and-health.md) on a real line: **this story
makes a route work; that one watches whether it still does.** Adding xcPINK is scoped, testable and
finished; *"Recheck XCM Weekly"* is neither.
